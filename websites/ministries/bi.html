Native Leaders Training Native Leaders

This short-term institute trains young people dedicated to full-time ministry. We believe in equipping tribal and local believers to preach the Gospel in their own languages and cultural contexts.

⏱️ Format

6 months classroom theology +
6 months field ministry practicum

🎯 Goal

Train pastors to plant churches in their own villages/towns/cities

💡 Philosophy

Native training native: saves finances, avoids cultural shock, multiplies impact

Why Invest in Native Leaders?

Comparison:

🇺🇸 Foreign Missionary

  • ~$6,000/year sustenance
  • Time lost fundraising
  • Cultural/financial adjustment
  • Language learning curve

🇮🇳 Native Pastor

  • $100/month support
  • Already culturally embedded
  • Speaks local language fluently
  • Long-term community presence

"It is better a Native trains Native—that saves much-needed finances and energy to be used in other fields for His Glory."
