Our Programs

☀️ Vacation Bible School (VBS)

When: Annual summer program
Where: Slums, villages, schools, hostels
Who: Ages 5-18, 99% non-Christian background

We share the Gospel through drawing, coloring, refreshments, and gifts. Every year, many children receive Christ as Savior.

⛺ Children's Residential Camps

Duration: 5 days
Participants: 500 children from slums, schools, Sunday schools
Cost: $5 per child for full 5-day camp

Many children attend without one full meal a day at home. We provide nutritious meals, Gospel teaching, counseling, and Christ's love. Many become pastors, evangelists, and full-time workers for the Lord.

Volunteer Opportunity: 1 teacher/counselor per 10 children needed during camps.

🏠 Home Bible Clubs

Format: 1-hour weekly meetings in Christian homes in slums
Locations: 15+ clubs across different areas
Impact: Children accept Christ, grow in faith, join church, and are baptized after age 18

🏫 School Bible Clubs

Conducted in Christian schools during assemblies and moral instruction classes, reaching many non-Christian children with the Gospel.

✊ Clubs for Rag Pickers

We conduct special clubs for children who collect recyclables to survive. We share the Gospel, provide food, and distribute clothing to needy families.

You can participate in this life-changing ministry!
