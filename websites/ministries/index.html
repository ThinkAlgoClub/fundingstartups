⛪ Church Planting Ministry

Current Impact: 7 worship locations • 1,900-2,000 believers gathering weekly

By the grace of our Lord, we have been sustained in planting churches where the name of Jesus Christ had not been heard. Behind every church are scars, sufferings, and sacrifices offered for His glory.

Current Needs

  • Permanent worship spaces: Many congregations meet in rented homes, donated land, or thatched huts where rain makes worship impossible.
  • Pastor support: Native pastors and their families face extreme poverty—children without proper clothing, studies, or winter wear.
  • Training resources: Equipping pastors with biblical teaching tools in Telugu and tribal languages.
"Pastors who really work for the Lord always suffer" — 2 Timothy 3:12

How You Can Help

Pray: For protection, provision, and fruitfulness for each church plant.

Give: Support a pastor's family needs: clothing, medicine, travel, church rent.

Partner: Sponsor a church planting initiative in an unreached village.
