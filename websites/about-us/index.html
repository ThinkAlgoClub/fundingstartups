Our Vision & Mission

🎯 Mission

Take the Gospel to places where the name of Jesus Christ has not been heard, through church planting and native leadership training.

🌱 Strategy

Train native pastors in their local language and cultural context to plant sustainable churches in their own communities.

❤️ Heart

Serve the poorest communities with compassion: children in slums, widows, HIV+ individuals, and orphaned youth.

Statement of Faith

We are: Fundamental Baptist, Independent, non-charismatic believers who love the Lord and His Word.

  • The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God
  • Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone
  • The Great Commission: making disciples of all nations
  • The priesthood of all believers and native leadership
  • Compassionate service to the marginalized and poor
